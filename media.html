    The Red Badge of Courage by Stephen Crane -- my favorite war book

    Walden by Henry David Thoreau-- everyone who cares about nature and craves ways to get away from society should read this

    The Royal Game by Stefan Zweig -- an epic novella about chess, showcasing the power of the mind
